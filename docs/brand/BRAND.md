# Nurby press kit

Assets and guidelines for writing about or linking to Nurby. Free to use when covering the project. Please do not alter the logo colours or proportions.

## What Nurby is (one line)

Free, open-source, self-hosted AI software that understands your security cameras with local vision models. It also includes Guardian, a privacy-first way for families to follow one person in a facility.

## Boilerplate (50 words)

Nurby is free, open-source, self-hosted software that reads camera feeds with local vision models, recognizes faces and license plates, captions scenes, runs plain-language automation rules, and answers questions about footage. With a local model, no video ever leaves your network. Guardian lets each family follow one specific person and nobody else.
